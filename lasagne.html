Quick And Easy Vegetable Lasagna

This vegetable lasagna recipe is a reader favorite! Tender vegetables, a light and flavorful tomato sauce, and lots of cheese make this the best veggie lasagna ever.

Ingredients For Delicious Vegetable Lasagne

  • No-boil noodles.
  • tomato sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Sea Salt
  • Egg

Here's A Step By Step Process For This Recipe.

  1. Make the simple tomato sauce, which takes about 20 minutes
  2. Combine ricotta cheese, egg, and a bit of salt together
  3. Assemble the lasagna by layering the vegetable sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded cheese.
  4. The lasagna bakes in the oven for about 35 minutes And Then It Is Ready To Be Served
